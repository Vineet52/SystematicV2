View Product

View Product

Purpose

This function displays the details of a product. It also allows the user to navigate to other functions relating to the product.


How it Works?

On the view product page, the details of the product are displayed for the user. This includes the name of the product, a brief description, what product type classifies the product, details about the price (cost price shows the price the products have been bought from the supplier, guide discount describes the suggested price after a discount has been given if deemed appropriate by the sales manager, and the selling price describes the amount at which the product should be sold without discount).

Products are bought and sold in three different size categories: palettes, cases and individually. The quantities of pallets, cases and individuals are displayed for the product.

To maintain the product, the user must click the        button. To delete a product, click the          button. To convert a pallet or case to a smaller unit, click the         button. To write products off, click the         button. To return to the previous screen, click thebutton at the bottom of the screen.
